Julia Chiang our CEO is the one of the founders of our company. She and one of Julia’s close friends decided to found this company, when Julia’s CTO’s grandpa died when he didn’t have the assistance when he needed it. This inspired her to start her company Reltub. Reltub designs and creates robotic butlers. These robotic butlers help men and woman, young and old around the house. These robots are programmed to fit the customer’s needs. Julia hopes that this product will help people in the ways that they need it to help them. Julia is 12 and she is working on building more factories around the world so that they can get the product all over the world.
Taylor Tam is the CTO of Reltub Co. She built the website and helps program the robotic butlers as well as run the factories that produce the parts to build our robotic butlers. Taylor loves to code. Along with this she also helped found the company and had the inspiration to start the company. She also helps to keep the company on track and working.
Matthew Choi is the COO of Reltub Co. He oversees ongoing business operations within the company, to see if he robots are doing all right. He is the second-in-command with the CEO, so he checks if there are robot butler activities going on in the company. Whenever something happens with the robots, he reports to the CEO and discusses the event. Along with reporting to the CEO he also works alongside Andrew to help him in marketing.
CMO is a marketing man that markets the product that you are selling. Andrew is in charge of advertising and marketing our services. Andrew is 11 and he is very excited to be expanding all around the world. Andrew came up with the logo and robot design. So he is our marketing man and also works on the design of the robotic butlers.
